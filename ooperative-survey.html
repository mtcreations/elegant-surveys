REROW Project

Turning Waste
Into Energy

Rural electric cooperatives sit at the intersection of agriculture, industry, and community. The REROW project helps cooperatives identify, evaluate, and develop waste-to-energy opportunities specific to their service area and member base.

Learn More
Cooperative Advisor Group +
The Cooperative Advisor Group (CAG) is a peer network of cooperative staff and board members actively engaged in waste-to-energy planning. Members provide real-world guidance to the REROW project team, shape research priorities, and gain early access to tools, findings, and funding opportunities. Participation requires approximately 4-6 hours per quarter and is open to cooperatives at any stage of exploration.
Waste Stream Analysis Program +
Participating cooperatives receive a no-cost technical assessment of the organic, agricultural, and industrial waste streams present in their service territory. Analysts characterize feedstock availability, energy conversion potential, and proximity to cooperative infrastructure. Results are provided as a confidential summary report and can inform board-level decisions, grant applications, or feasibility studies.
